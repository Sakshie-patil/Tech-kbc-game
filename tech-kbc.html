TECH-KBC

Description:

Welcome to Tech-KBC! Test your knowledge of technology with a fun and challenging quiz! Answer multiple-choice questions, and see how many you can get right. With each level, the difficulty increases, and you can use lifelines to help you along the way. Ready to challenge yourself? Start the game now!

and rules :

1] You will be asked a series of multiple-choice questions on technology.

2] There is no time limit constraint to answer the question.

3] Lifelines: You have 2 lifelines to help you out:
a] 50:50: Remove two incorrect options.
b] Swap the Question : swap current question with random one.

4] As you answer correctly, you move up to higher levels with tougher questions.

5] If you choose the wrong answer, the game ends, but you can always try again!
